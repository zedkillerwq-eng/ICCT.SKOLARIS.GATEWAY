BS Information Technology Program

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program prepares students to become IT professionals who can assist organizations in solving problems using information technology. The program focuses on the study of the utilization of computers and computer software to plan, install, customize, operate, manage, administer, and maintain information technology infrastructure.

Career Opportunities

  • IT Project Manager
  • Systems Analyst
  • Network Administrator
  • Database Administrator
  • Web Developer
  • Software Engineer
  • IT Consultant

Program Outcomes

  1. Analyze complex problems and apply principles of computing and other relevant disciplines to identify solutions
  2. Design, implement, and evaluate computer-based systems, processes, components, or programs to meet desired needs and requirements
  3. Apply mathematical foundations, algorithmic principles, and computer science theory in the modeling and design of computer-based systems
  4. Use current techniques, skills, and tools necessary for computing practice
  5. Apply knowledge through the use of current tools of the discipline

Dashboard Help & Support

Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my requirements?

To upload requirements, go to the Requirements section, click on the "Upload" button next to the requirement you need to submit, select the file from your device, and confirm the upload. Make sure your file is in PDF format and does not exceed 5MB in size.

How can I view my grades?

Your grades can be viewed in the Grades & Records section. You can see your current term grades as well as your academic history. Final grades are typically posted within 2 weeks after the final examination period.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept GCash, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and bank transfers. You can make payments through the Account & Payments section of your portal. For cash payments, you can visit the Accounting Office during business hours.

How do I request for an official transcript?

Official transcript requests can be made through the Registrar's Office. You need to complete the transcript request form, settle the required fees, and present a valid ID. Processing typically takes 3-5 business days.
